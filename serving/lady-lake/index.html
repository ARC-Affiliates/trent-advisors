#What is the 90 Day Rule in Florida Insurance?

Florida's 90-day rule generally refers to the grace period and notice requirements insurers must follow before canceling certain policies for nonpayment, giving policyholders time to cure a lapse. Always confirm specifics with the [Florida Office of Insurance Regulation](https://floir.com/).
