Q.What Is the Difference Between Medicare Supplement and Medicare Advantage?
Medicare Supplement is private insurance that pays the gaps Original Medicare leaves behind. Medicare Advantage is an alternative way to get your Medicare benefits through a private plan that replaces Original Medicare.
